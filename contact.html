Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service through Rudzz Auto?

To book a service, search for providers in your area, select a provider, and click the "Book Appointment" button on their profile. Follow the steps to select your service, preferred date and time, and provide your contact information.

How do I become a service provider on Rudzz Auto?

To join as a service provider, click on "Join as Provider" in the footer or go to the signup page and select "Service Provider" as your account type. Complete the registration process, including business details, services offered, and verification documents.

Is there a fee to use Rudzz Auto?

Rudzz Auto is free for car owners to use. Service providers pay a small commission on completed bookings. There are no upfront fees or monthly subscriptions for basic provider accounts.

How are service providers verified?

We verify service providers through a thorough process that includes checking business licenses, professional certifications, identity verification, and customer reviews. Providers with a verified badge have completed this process.
